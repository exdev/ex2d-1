Can't pick the H-Fliped or V-Fliped sprite in the scene

Unity3D's picking algorithm is affect by culling mode. So when you Flip the sprite, the vertex draw order changes, our sprite shader use Culling Off settings, but it looks like the picking still affect by culling.

Don't save the Atlas and Sprite Animation with the same name in the same folder

Since the Atlas and Sprite Animation all have the suffix (.asset), you must carefully named these two assets. We suggest you save them in different directory. But if they are in same directory, please make sure they have different name or your Atlas or Sprite Animation data will be overwrite.
